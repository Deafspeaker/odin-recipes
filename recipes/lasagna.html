Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Boxed Lasagna Noodles
  • Pasta Sauce
  • 1lb Ground Beef
  • Vegetables
  • Cottage Cheese
  • Mozzarella Cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven to 450 degrees.
  2. Brown the Ground Beef in a pan.
  3. Chop Vegetables
  4. Drain grease from ground beef and return to pan.
  5. Add Vegetables and pasta sauce to pan with
    ground beef and simmer for 10 miunutes.
  6. Grab your cassarole dish and start with a
    layer of your pan mix, followed by a layer
    of cottage cheese. Repeate until you get to
    top of your dish, or run out of ingredients.
  7. Place dish in oven and cook for about an hour
  8. Place as much as you want of mazzarella
    for a top layer.
  9. Cook for an additional 15-30 minutes until
    cheese is golden brown.
  10. Take it out and let rest before serving.
